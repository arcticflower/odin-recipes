Pasta Alla Vodka

Try our easy pasta all vodka recipe today.

Ingredients

  • 16 oz Pasta
  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Vodka

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Melt butter in pan and cook tomatos on medium heat.
  3. Add 3/4 cups vodka and stir until alcohol cooks off.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and stir.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
