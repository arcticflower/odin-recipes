Margherita Pizza

Our recipe for margherita pizza will simply blow your mind.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Tomato Paste
  • Oregano
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. Roll the pizza dough flat.
  2. Spread tomato paste evenly across dough.
  3. Sprinkle with oregano.
  4. Cover pie with mozzarella cheese.
